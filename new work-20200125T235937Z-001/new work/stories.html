SUMMARY OF IMPACT

A Little Something is a social enterprise model centred around economic and environmental sustainability. It involves three aspects: a cooking class, a catering program and craft. Our cooking class will offer more than just the opportunity to create delicious dishes from new settler communities. It also:

  • Uses superfoods as ingredients to improve community health
  • Raises awareness about the importance of a clean environment by teaching participants to effectively utilise food scraps to create compost
  • Reduces effects of fast fashion by allowing the participants to repurpose old shirts into handy products, like tote bags

The new settler women also run a mini pop up stall, where they sell beautiful products, such as jewellery, that they have created using donated or recycled materials (donated by Vinnies)

The use of recycled materials brings attention to the detrimental impact of plastics on the environment and offers an alternative option that also allows the women to help support themselves financially.
